## What is Change Password?

Users can change their account password directly from the Supply Now app without contacting support.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I change my password?

**A:** Follow these steps:

1. Open the **Supply Now** app
2. Go to the **Profile** tab (bottom navigation)
3. In the **Personal** tab, tap **Change Password**
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter a new password and confirm it
6. Tap **Save**

### Q: What are the password requirements?

**A:** Your new password must:

- Be at least **8 characters** long
- Contain at least one **letter** and one **number**
- Match the confirmation field exactly

### Q: I forgot my current password. Can I still change it?

**A:** No, you need your current password to change it in the app. If you forgot your password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login screen to reset it via email.

### Q: I get "Current password is incorrect". What should I do?

**A:** Make sure you are typing your **current** (old) password in the first field, not the new one. Check for caps lock and extra spaces.

### Q: My password was changed but I got logged out. Is that normal?

**A:** Your session may remain active after a password change. If you are logged out, simply log in again with your new password.

## Common Issues

### Issue: "Passwords do not match"

**Symptoms:** Error message when trying to save new password.
**Cause:** The "New Password" and "Confirm Password" fields have different values.
**Solution:** Carefully re-type the new password in both fields, making sure they match exactly.

### Issue: "Password must contain letters and numbers"

**Symptoms:** Error message when trying to save new password.
**Cause:** The new password contains only letters or only numbers.
**Solution:** Add at least one number if your password is all letters, or add at least one letter if it's all numbers.
